How it works

A simple process from idea to finished site.

01

Tell me what you need

Email me what kind of website you need and what you want it to include.

02

I build the site

I design and build the website based on what you send me.

03

We launch it

Once it is ready, I help get it online and connected to a domain if needed.

What to send

The more details you send, the better I can build your site.

01

Brand and site basics

Send your business or brand name, what your website is about, what you offer, and what you want visitors to do.

02

Style and colors

Tell me your color scheme, the vibe you want, and any example websites or links you like.

03

Pages and content

List the tabs/pages you need, like Home, About, Services, Gallery, Booking, Contact, or anything custom.

04

Photos and placement

Send photos, logos, videos, or graphics you want used, and tell me where you want them on the site.

05

Hosting choice

Tell me if you are fine using a GitHub website or if you want to purchase your own domain for a branded link.

06

Domain help

If you buy your own domain, I can help copy the code over and connect the finished site after purchase.
